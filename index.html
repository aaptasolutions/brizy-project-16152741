Work

with us

Content

Editor

Create and manage the content for our clients' websites or other digital platforms. This includes planning, creating, publishing, and promoting content to attract and engage audiences. You must also analyze metrics and adjust strategies as necessary to improve performance and meet business goals.

Outbound Sales

Make international calls to identify potential customers, develop and maintain relationships with prospects, and ultimately close deals. You must have excellent communication & negotiation skills and be able to handle objections and rejections professionally while consistently meeting or exceeding sales targets.

Admin Executive

Provide administrative support to the management, manage office operations and ensuring smooth running of the office. This includes managing calendars, coordinating meetings, handling correspondences, managing databases and filing systems. You must be organized, detail-oriented, and able to multitask effectively.
